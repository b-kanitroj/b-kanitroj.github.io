Project

This project examines the relationship between infrastructure disparities (e.g., road density, healthcare access) and societal challenges such as health, education, and employment issues in Thailand. By visualizing these relationships through interactive charts and maps, the analysis identifies underserved provinces and highlights the compounded burdens faced by communities with inadequate infrastructure. The findings aim to provide actionable insights for policymakers, advocating for targeted investments and data-driven approaches to welfare reform.
